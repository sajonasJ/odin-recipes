Grilled Salmon

Let's you swim with dolphins

Ingredients

  • Filet of Salmon
  • Any cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to 200 degrees this takes about 10 minutes.
  2. Grill the Salmon to 200-250 degrees.
  3. Oil the fish not the grill.
  4. Flip the salmon 90% of the cooking time.
